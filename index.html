About

"Six years ago, I picked up a camera not to capture the world —
but to understand it."

Six years behind the lens has taught me that beauty isn't staged — it's found. Based in the heart of Mysore, my work is a tribute to simplicity: the quiet emotion in a hand-woven fabric, the honest steam rising from a morning brew. I believe every product has a pulse, every person carries a story that deserves to be told without pretence.

I don't just photograph — I collaborate. With local makers, small businesses, and visionaries who care deeply about what they create. Together, we turn their craft into something that lasts far longer than a trend.

Let's meet over coffee. Let's make something real.
